## What to Include

To help us assist you faster, please include:

- Your shop name
- Your account email address
- A clear description of your question or issue
- Screenshots if relevant (especially for technical issues)

## Feature Requests

Bike Yard is built for bike shops, and we actively listen to feedback. If there's a feature that would make your life easier, let us know!

Email your ideas to **support@bikeyard.com.au** with "Feature Request" in the subject line.

---

## Report an Issue

Found a bug or something not working right? Please email us with:

1. What you were trying to do
2. What happened instead
3. Your browser and device (e.g., "Chrome on Windows" or "Safari on iPhone")
4. Screenshots if possible
