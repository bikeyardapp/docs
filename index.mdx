## What is Bike Yard?

Bike Yard connects bike shops with buyers actively searching for bikes. Unlike marketplace sites, we're a **lead generation platform** — buyers browse your listings and contact you directly.

**No transaction fees. No commissions. Just quality leads.**

### Key Features

| Feature                     | Benefit                                               |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **99spokes Integration**    | Add bikes in under 1 minute with auto-populated specs |
| **Direct Enquiries**        | Buyers contact you directly — no middleman            |
| **Analytics**               | Track views and enquiries for every listing           |
| **Google My Business Sync** | Import shop details and ratings instantly             |
