## 6. Listing Requirements and Standards

### 6.1 Accurate Listings

All bike listings must accurately represent:

- Bikes that are genuinely available for purchase;
- Current pricing;
- Actual condition of the bike (new, ex-demo, used);
- Available sizes and colours; and
- Stock availability status.

### 6.2 Prohibited Listings

You must NOT list:

- Bikes you do not have available and on-hand to sell;
- Stolen goods or goods obtained illegally;
- Counterfeit products or products that infringe intellectual property rights;
- Products that do not comply with Australian safety standards;
- Items that are not bicycles or directly related bicycle products; or
- Listings primarily intended to drive traffic to external sites without genuine intent to sell the listed bike.

### 6.3 Timely Updates

You must:

- Update or remove listings promptly when bikes are sold;
- Keep pricing current and accurate;
- Respond to buyer enquiries in a timely manner; and
- Maintain accurate shop profile information.

### 6.4 Listing Integrity Policy

To maintain the quality and trustworthiness of the Bike Yard marketplace, we enforce a strict policy against misleading listings. This includes, but is not limited to:

- Listing bicycles that are not genuinely available for sale at the time of listing;
- Advertising bicycles at prices significantly below the actual selling price with the intent to attract enquiries (commonly known as "bait pricing");
- Any other conduct designed to mislead potential buyers or artificially generate enquiries.

**'Three Stikes' Enforcement**

Where we determine, at our sole discretion, that a Subscriber has breached this Listing Integrity Policy, the following consequences will apply:

(a) **First Breach:** A formal written warning will be issued to the Subscriber. The offending listing(s) will be removed.

(b) **Second Breach:** The Subscriber's account will be suspended for a period of seven (7) days. During suspension, all listings will be hidden from public view and the Subscriber will be unable to create new listings or receive enquiries.

(c) **Third Breach:** The Subscriber's account will be suspended for a period of one (1) calendar month. During suspension, all listings will be hidden from public view and the Subscriber will be unable to create new listings or receive enquiries.

(d) **Subsequent Breaches:** We reserve the right to terminate the Subscriber's account in accordance with clause 9 (Termination).

For the avoidance of doubt, subscription fees remain payable during any period of account suspension and no refunds or credits will be issued for suspension periods resulting from breaches of this policy.
