## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Correction

Under the Privacy Act, you have the right to:

- Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@bikeyard.com.au.

### 6.2 Account Information

You can access, update, or delete certain account information through your dashboard at any time, including:

- Contact details
- Shop profile information
- Bike listings
- Communication preferences

### 6.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by:

- Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in marketing emails
- Updating your communication preferences in your account settings
- Contacting us at support@bikeyard.com.au

Note: You cannot opt out of service-related communications (account notifications, security alerts, billing information).

### 6.4 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account by contacting us at support@bikeyard.com.au. Upon account deletion:

- Your shop profile and listings will be removed from public view
- We will delete your personal information, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (see Section 7)

### 6.5 Complaints

If you believe we have breached the APPs or this Privacy Policy, you may lodge a complaint with us at support@bikeyard.com.au. We will investigate and respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

### 8.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

### 8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies:**
- Required for the Service to function
- Enable secure login and account access
- Cannot be disabled

**Analytics Cookies:**
- Help us understand how visitors use the Service
- Collect anonymised usage statistics
- Provided by Google Analytics

**Preference Cookies:**
- Remember your settings and preferences
- Improve your experience on return visits

### 8.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.

Most browsers allow you to:
- View cookies stored on your device
- Delete all or specific cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies

### 8.4 Do Not Track

We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals. However, you can manage your privacy preferences through cookie settings as described above.
